Aperture Alchemy

Aperture Alchemy (Photography): A photography contest themed "Amidst the Rush," tasking students with capturing unedited images that show stillness within a chaotic environment.

Theme and Overview

Theme: Amidst the Rush

In a world constantly rushing forward, the theme captures moments of calm within chaos. It invites photographers to freeze time amid motion, highlighting individuals, emotions, or elements that remain steady despite the surrounding blur of activity.

Task

  • Submit one original photograph relevant to the "Amidst the Rush" theme.

  • Participants must explore the contrast between movement and stillness.

  • Assign a suitable caption to the photograph.

Allowed Tools and Resources

  • Any digital camera or smartphone.

  • Software Restriction: The photograph must NOT be edited using any software or digital tools.

  • Format: File must be submitted in .png format.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Single round (Online).

  • Entries must be original work; plagiarism leads to immediate disqualification.

  • Ethics: Ask permission before photographing individuals and respect the subject's context.

  • Submit the entry via a Google Drive link to the host school.

Judgement Criteria

  • Originality and Creativity

  • Relevance to the Topic

  • Quality of Content

  • Visual Appeal
